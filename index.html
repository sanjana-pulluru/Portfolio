Ziguzagu Sudoku Solver & Generator

Ziguzagu Sudoku Solver & Generator is an innovative Python project aimed at revolutionizing the Sudoku puzzle-solving experience. Named after the intriguing zigzag patterns that emerge within the game, this project offers a unique approach to solving and generating Sudoku puzzles.

Heart Stroke Prediction using Machine Learning

Our Heart Stroke Prediction project leverages the power of machine learning algorithms to accurately predict the likelihood of an individual experiencing a heart stroke. Heart stroke, a life-threatening medical emergency, can be prevented if detected early. This project aims to provide a proactive approach to healthcare by predicting the risk of heart stroke based on various health parameters and lifestyle factors.
